# Week 1 - Capstone Lane Selection

## Selected Lane
SEO Page Performance Prediction

## Search Question
Can we predict which webpages are likely to receive high organic traffic using SEO metrics?

## Unit of Analysis
Each webpage in the dataset is treated as one unit of analysis.

## Expected Output
The machine learning model will predict whether a webpage is likely to have high or low organic traffic.

## Action
Based on the prediction, the SEO team can prioritize improving low-performing webpages and maintain high-performing ones.

## Cost of Wrong Recommendation
If the model predicts incorrectly, the team may spend time and resources optimizing the wrong webpages, leading to reduced SEO performance and missed traffic opportunities.

## Why Machine Learning?
Machine learning can analyze multiple SEO metrics simultaneously and identify patterns that are difficult to detect manually. This helps make more accurate and data-driven decisions.
